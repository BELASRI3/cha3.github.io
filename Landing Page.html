Welcome to Lisbon !

Lisbon is one of the most beautiful cities in Europe, with picturesque charm, rich history, and breathtaking views. This city located on the banks of the Tagus offers a perfect blend of tradition and modernity, with old trams alongside modern buildings and cobbled squares overlooking green parks.

Presentation of the city

The Christ King

Is a national sanctuary of the Catholic Church and religious monument representing the Sacred Heart of Jesus. It is located in the municipality of Almada in Portugal. Overlooking the 25-April Bridge, it faces the city of Lisbon across the mouth of the Tagus River.

The Belem Tower

In the Manueline style, is a work by Francisco de Arruda that was built between 1515 and 1519. In 1983, it was declared a Cultural Heritage of Humanity by UNESCO. It is located very close to the Hieronymites Monastery.

Yellow Tram

The Lisbon Tram 28 passes through the popular tourist neighborhoods of Graca, Alfama, Baixa, and Estrela. For visitors, it's the classic tram ride in Lisbon, taking the historic yellow tram that winds through the city's narrow streets. Tram number 28 passes through several of Lisbon's most interesting neighborhoods.
